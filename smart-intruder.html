RHAMP AND ARSATA

Road Hazard Alert Masterpiece (RHAMP) and Audible Road Signs and Traffic Alert (ARSATA) RHAMP is the mobile app and arsata is the device

Description

Research carried out by the RAC, presented in their annual Report on Motoring for 2014, suggests that drivers are failing to maintain their knowledge of the rules of the road after passing their driving test. In most cases, they forget due to lack of guidance information which is made available during driving lessons. RHAMP stands as the mobile app counterpart to the Audible Road Signs and Traffic Alert (ARSATA) system. They are cutting-edge systems meticulously designed to enhance driver safety by intelligently identifying and plotting road signs and alerting drivers about potential hazards on the road. This innovative system is engineered to effortlessly identify road signs, curves, and unexpected hazards without distracting drivers, ensuring a heightened level of safety on the roads. This also effectively overcome obstacles such as poor visibility due to fog, heavy rain, snowfall, and other extreme weather conditions. By mitigating the possibility of missed road signs, the system significantly reduces the occurrence of preventable road accidents.

Features

  • Instant Road Sign Voice notification
  • Plotting of Road Signs
  • Timely Hazard Voice Alerts
  • Offline Functionality
  • Universal car Compatibility and Installation
  • Multi-Language Support
  • Driving Data Analysis
  • Triangular shaped device

Impact

Reduced Accidents, Improved Safety, Driver Skill Enhancement
